Welcome To Alevit

Alevit Construction and Maintance Services offers a dependable solution to home repair and remodeling in Sydney at its suburbs.

We are fast and provide non-obligation free quotes in your home, business or industrial premises. We provide our services at a discounted price which makes it worth your while, even to call us for a small size job. We want your business so we will quote the correct price to win the job.

We are always working in and around Sydney with local property managers and real estates, so we know what needs to be fixed in most of the buildings and older style cottages.

We are licensed carpenters, security installers and maintance service providers. We will always be able to put you in touch with a specialist, such as a fully licensed local locksmith, plumber, electrician etc.
